Fruit Tree



Orange

Location: Hall 9
Scientific Name: Citrus sinensis
Uses: Protects your cells from damage, Helps your body make collagen, a protein that heals wounds and gives you smoother skin, Makes it easier to absorb iron to fight anemia, Boosts your immune system, your body's defense against germs.

Nagami Kumquat

Location: Hall 12
Scientific Name: Citrus Margarita
Uses: Kumquats have about 13 calories each. That’s about 90 calories a serving. They’re high in vitamins C (about 8 mg each) and offer some vitamin A (about 3 mcg each). Kumquats are useful in vision, growth, reproduction.

Banana

Location: Hall 12
Scientific Name: Musa Sp.
Uses: Banana helps to improve heart health, helps to build lean muscles, supports bone health, helps muscles to recover after exercise helps to reduce bloating.

Guava

Location: Hall 12
Scientific Name: Psidium Guajava
Uses: Various parts of the plant, including the leaf and the fruit, are used as medicine. People use guava leaf for stomach and intestinal conditions, pain, diabetes, and wound healing. The fruit is used for high blood pressure.

Sugar Apple

Location: Hall 12
Scientific Name: Annona Squamosa
Uses: It's rich in minerals like iron, manganese, magnesium, calcium, potassium, and phosphorus. Among vitamins, it has significant amounts of vitamin A, B1, B2, B3, B5, B6, and B9 as well as slightly more vitamin C than grapefruit. It's also an excellent source of phytochemicals and antioxidants.

Acovado

Location: Hall 10
Scientific Name: Persea americana
Uses: It's also rich in monounsaturated fats and is a good source of linoleic acid. avocado is also used for high cholesterol. It is also used for memory and thinking skills, aging skin, obesity, psoriasis, heart disease, and many other purposes.
